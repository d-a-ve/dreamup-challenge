About DreamUp

The DreamUp challenge is a 4 week online challenge where participants will be grouped into teams to learn HTML & CSS from w3schools.

A quiz will be held every week for the 4 weeks where the winning team and runnerup team will win prizes for their performance.

UI Design Talks

UI(User Interface) design considers the look, feel, and interactivity of websites or web apps. It is all about making sure that the user interface of a product is as intuitive as possible, and that means carefully considering each and every visual, interactive element the user might encounter.

Web3 Talks

Web3 (also known as Web 3.0) is an idea for a new iteration of the World Wide Web which incorporates concepts such as decentralization, blockchain technologies, and token-based economics.

Financial Talks

Health Talks

"Health is wealth". Your health is very important so you need to take care of yourself mentally and physically. Being a techie in the 21st Century is not easy as you will constantly be staring at your screen to solve a problem or brainstroming with your team. This could have an adverse effect on your body in the long term, so we will be talking about ways to stay healthy while also working.
